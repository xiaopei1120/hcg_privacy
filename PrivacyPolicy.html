Privacy Policy
Nuoqi’s SeasideShop

📄 Version 1.0 📅 Effective: May 20, 2026
🌍 GDPR | UK GDPR | CCPA/CPRA | COPPA | Global Privacy Framework

Nuoqi’s SeasideShop complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), UK GDPR, California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and other global privacy laws. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account info: username, email address, password, date of birth (for age verification).
  • Customer support: messages, screenshots, account details.
  • Payment data: transaction ID, timestamp, amount (processed by Apple/Google/Payment Providers; we do not store full card details).

B. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device: model, OS version, unique device identifiers (IDFA, Android ID, GAID), IP address, MAC address.
  • Game data: login time, session duration, level progress, merge actions, in-game purchases, crash logs, gameplay behavior.
  • Location: approximate region (from IP) for language/region settings (no precise location).

C. Third-Party SDKs

We use SDKs for analytics, ads, push notifications, and cloud saves. These may collect:

  • IDFA/GAID, IP, device info, app usage.
  • Providers may include:
  • Analytics: Firebase, Adjust, AppsFlyer
  • Ads: AdMob, Unity Ads, IronSource
  • Push: Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Authentication: Apple Sign In, Google Sign In

2. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

We process data based on:

  • Consent: for personalized ads, marketing, analytics.
  • Contract: to provide game services, account management, purchases.
  • Legitimate interests: security, anti-cheat, fraud prevention, service improvement.
  • Legal obligation: compliance with laws (e.g., age verification, anti-money laundering).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Game.
  • Authenticate accounts and prevent fraud/cheating.
  • Process purchases and deliver virtual items.
  • Send game updates, offers, and support messages.
  • Analyze usage to optimize gameplay and performance.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share data only:

  • With service providers (payment, analytics, ads) under strict confidentiality.
  • When required by law, court order, or government request.
  • With your explicit consent.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice).
✧ We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

5. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to countries outside the EEA/UK. We ensure protection via:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
  • Adequacy decisions (e.g., to countries approved by the EU Commission).

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: until account deletion.
  • Game logs: 12–24 months (anonymized thereafter).
  • Payment records: as required by tax/consumer law.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA / CPRA)

You may request:

  • Access: copies of your personal data.
  • Correction: inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: your account and data (right to be forgotten).
  • Restriction: limit processing.
  • Data portability: receive data in machine-readable form.
  • Withdraw consent: for ads/analytics/marketing.
  • Opt-out of sale: none (we do not sell data).

  • If you are a parent/guardian and believe we have data from your child, contact us to delete it.

9. Security

We use encryption, access controls, and secure servers to protect data. No method is 100% secure; you are responsible for account security.

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified in-game or via email.
