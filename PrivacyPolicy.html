Privacy Policy
Nuoqi’s Seaside Shop

📄 Version 1.0 📅 Effective Date: June 5, 2026

Nuoqi’s Seaside Shop is a casual merge game developed by Qinglan Technology (hereinafter referred to as “we”, “us”, or “our company”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

To save your game progress (e.g., merge levels, items owned), we need to store relevant data on our servers. Specifically, we collect:

  • Game progress data: your merge history, level progress, virtual items, etc.
  • Device identifier: we may use your device’s Android ID or a generated unique user ID to associate your saved game data, allowing you to restore progress after reinstalling the app or switching devices.
  • Network information: your IP address is temporarily recorded in server logs for security and troubleshooting purposes, and we regularly delete these logs.

We do NOT collect: your real name, email address (unless you voluntarily contact customer support), precise location, contacts, photos, files, or any other sensitive information unrelated to the game.

2. No Third-Party SDKs

The current version of this game does not integrate any third‑party analytics, advertising, push notification, or crash reporting SDKs. All data (game saves) are transmitted only to our own servers and are not shared with any third‑party companies.

If we add advertising or analytics features in the future, we will update this privacy policy and notify you via Google Play release notes.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

As long as you use the game, we will retain your game progress data. If you wish to delete your data, please follow the steps below:

  • In‑app account deletion: Open the game → Settings → Privacy Management → Tap “Cancel Account”. After deletion, all your game progress data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Please note that account deletion is irreversible, and your data cannot be recovered once deleted.

4. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA, if applicable), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate data;
  • Request deletion of your data (using the in‑app deletion feature above);
  • Restrict or object to certain processing of your data.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to our data collection practices, we will notify you via in‑game notice or through Google Play release notes.
